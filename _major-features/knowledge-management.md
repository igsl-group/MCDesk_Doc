# 7. Knowledge Management

**Capturing and Sharing Organizational Knowledge**

## Major Features

| Feature | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Knowledge Base Creation & Curation** | Tools to create, organize, and manage articles, FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and best practices. |
| **Powerful search and tagging capabilities** | Powerful search capabilities to help users and agents find relevant information quickly. |
| **Version control and approval workflows for content** | Tools to manage the lifecycle of knowledge articles, including revisions and review processes. |
| **Feedback Mechanisms** | Allowing users to rate articles or provide feedback for ongoing improvement. |
| **Integration with the service desk for suggested articles during ticket logging** | |

## ITIL 4 Process Alignment

| Practice | Alignment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Knowledge Management** | The core function of this process is to enable the organization to capture, share, and leverage its knowledge. Directly supports the goal of maintaining and improving the quality of knowledge and information. |
| **Incident Management, Problem Management, Service Request Management** | A well-maintained knowledge base is crucial for faster resolution, self-service, and improved user experience in these processes. |
| **Continual Improvement** | Knowledge sharing is a key enabler of learning and improvement. |
| **Service Value Chain - Plan, Improve, Deliver & Support** | Ensures staff and users have the information they need to perform effectively. |
