# 6. Asset Management

**Tracking the Lifecycle of IT Assets**

## Major Features

| Feature | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Lifecycle Management** | Managing assets from procurement to retirement. |
| **Software Asset Management (SAM) & License Management** | Monitors software usage and manages license entitlements to ensure compliance and reduce costs. Tracking software licenses to ensure compliance and optimize usage. |
| **Hardware Asset Management (HAM) & Asset Tracking** | Tracks maintenance contracts, warranties, and lease agreements with automated renewal alerts. Monitoring asset location, status, ownership, and warranty information. |
| **Contract Management** | Tracks maintenance contracts, warranties, and lease agreements with automated renewal alerts. |
| **Cost Management & Depreciation / Financial Tracking** | Calculates the financial value of assets over time and manages total cost of ownership (TCO). Recording asset costs, depreciation, and disposal values. |
| **Purchasing & Requisition** | Integrates with procurement workflows for ordering hardware and software. |
| **Mobile Asset Tracking** | Mobile-enabled interface for technicians to update asset records and scan barcodes/QR codes in the field. |
| **Reporting & Dashboards** | Built-in analytics for tracking asset utilization and spend. |
| **Close Integration with CMDB** | |

## ITIL 4 Process / Practice Alignment

| Practice | Alignment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Asset Management** | Directly supports this process by providing tools to manage the lifecycle and costs of IT assets. |
| **Financial Management for IT Services** | Provides data for cost allocation and financial reporting. |
| **Service Design & Continual Improvement** | Understanding asset utilization and costs informs future design decisions and improvement initiatives. |
