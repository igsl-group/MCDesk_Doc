# Identify versions and license

To identify the MCdesk version and license, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig14.png)

2. Go to **[System Management]** > **[Versions and license]**. On the right pane, the system version and license key will be displayed.

   ![Versions and license]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig15.png)

You may need to verify the system version during communication with Support.
