# Create Announcement

To create an announcement in MCdesk, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig19.png)

2. Go to **[System Management]** > **[Announcement]**, then click **[New]** on the top-right corner to create a new announcement.

   ![User Management > Announcement]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig20.png)

3. Enter the mandatory fields as below:
   - **Name**
   - **Display order**
   - **Title**
   - **Start time**
4. Type in the content in the **Summary** and determine whether the **Active** boolean should be turned on or not.

   ![Create Announcement â€“ Summary and Active]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig21.png)

5. Click **[Submit]** to save the announcement.

The announcement will be shown to users according to the display order and active period you configured.
