# Create New Group

To create a group in MCdesk, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig1.png)

2. Go to **[User Management]** > **[Group]**, then click **[New]** on the top-right corner to create a group.

   ![User Management > Group]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig2.png)

3. Enter the mandatory fields as below:
   - **Name**

   ![Group â€“ Name field]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig3.png)

4. Click **[Save]** to save the group.

After creating the group, you can assign users, other groups, and roles to it as needed.
