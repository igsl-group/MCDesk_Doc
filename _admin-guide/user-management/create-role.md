# Create New Role

To create a role in MCdesk, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig7.png)

2. Go to **[User Management]** > **[Role]**, then click **[New]** on the top-right corner to create a role.

   ![User Management > Role]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig8.png)

3. Enter the mandatory fields as below:
   - **Name**

   ![Role – Name field]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig9.png)

4. Click **[Save]** to save the role.

## Assign users to the role

After saving, you can add users to this role:

1. Click the **[Assign/Unassign]** button in the **[Contain Users]** tab under **[Related Links]**.

   ![Contain Users – Assign/Unassign]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig10.png)

2. In the **[Assign]** window, choose the user account(s) on the **[Source]** side and then click the **[>]** button to move them to the **[Target]** side.

   ![Assign window – users]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig11.png)

3. Click **[Save]** after choosing all the target users.

## Assign roles to the role

1. Next, click the **[Contain Roles]** tab and click the **[Assign/Unassign]** button.

   ![Contain Roles tab]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig12.png)

2. In the **[Assign]** window, choose the role(s) on the **[Source]** side and then click the **[>]** button to move them to the **[Target]** side.

   ![Assign window – roles]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/admin-guide-fig13.png)

3. Click **[Save]** after choosing all the target roles.
