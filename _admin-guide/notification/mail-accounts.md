# Configure Mail Accounts

To configure email accounts in MCdesk, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig1.png)

2. Go to **[Email Settings]** > **[Mail Accounts]**, then click **[New]** on the top-right corner to create a new mail account.

   ![Email Settings > Mail Accounts]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig2.png)

3. Enter the mandatory fields as below:
   - **Authentication type**
   - **Name**
   - **Username**
   - **Type**
4. Fill in other fields as required, including **Password**, **Connection security**, **Server**, and **Port**.

   ![Mail Account â€“ fields]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig3.png)

5. Click **[Save]** to save the Mail Account details.
