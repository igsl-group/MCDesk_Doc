# Create New Notification Rule

To create a new notification rule in MCdesk, follow the steps below.

## Steps

1. Click on **[System Setting]** from the menu.

   ![System Setting menu]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig7.png)

2. Go to **[Email Settings]** > **[Notification Rule]**, then click **[New]** on the top-right corner to create a new notification rule.

   ![Email Settings > Notification Rule]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig8.png)

3. Enter the mandatory fields as below:
   - **Rule name**
   - **Table name**
4. Fill in other fields as required, including when the notification would be sent, the target party to receive it, and the notification template to use.

   ![Notification Rule â€“ fields]({{site.url}}{{site.baseurl}}/images/admin-guide/notification-fig9.png)

5. Click **[Submit]** to save the notification rule.
